Who We Are

Team Omega includes Aaron Johnson, Megan Morrison, and Rachel Schlick who were senior undergraduate students at Oregon State University in Spring 2021.

Capstone Project Description

The Top-n Musical Genre Classification project utilized a convolutional neural network to train a musical genre classification model. We used the Free Music Archive (FMA) small dataset which includes 8,000 songs from 8 genres. We built and iterated on the initial model classifying mel-spectrograms using Jupyter Notebooks, serialized the model, and uploaded to the backend AWS hosted by elastic beanstalk. The predictions are displayed on the home page after a user uploads an .mp3 or .wav file.
